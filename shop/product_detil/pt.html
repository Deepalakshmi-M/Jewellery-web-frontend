Care Instructions

  1. Store Properly: Keep your jewelry in a dry, cool place. Use a soft cloth or a jewelry box with compartments to prevent scratching and tangling.
  2. Avoid Chemicals: Keep your jewelry away from harsh chemicals, including cleaning products, perfumes, and lotions.
  3. Limit Exposure: Remove jewelry before swimming, exercising, or engaging in activities that may cause it to get scratched or damaged.
  4. Clean Gently: Clean your jewelry regularly using a soft cloth. For more thorough cleaning, use mild soap and warm water.
  5. Check for Damage: Regularly inspect your jewelry for any signs of wear or damage, such as loose stones or clasps.
  6. Avoid Moisture: Keep jewelry away from moisture and humidity, which can cause tarnishing, especially with sterling silver.
